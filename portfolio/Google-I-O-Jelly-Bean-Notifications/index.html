Google I/O Jelly Bean Notifications

ui design · ux design

UI & UX design for Google IO Jelly Bean Notification Center demonstation during the keynote. Google came to TuneIn and asked it to be part of the presentation, so we were tasked with coming up with the simplest and most usable version of our app to be presented in the notification center.

We spent several days throwing ideas around, trying to decide on what we could include - everything from a favorites list to search to browse. In the end we decided to keep it simple: focus on on the currently playing artist, song or station. The ability to stop and favorite that track, and then, if you don't like it, to skip to a related station and start something new.

It was incredibly exciting to see the design come to life and then be presented on stage at IO.
