Caavo Control Center

product · ux · strategy

As Principal Product Manager at Caavo, I led the software experience for the Control Center — the world's smartest 4K HDMI switch paired with an intuitive universal remote designed to simplify everything connected to your TV. I owned the full product experience from concept through launch, working across hardware, software, and content partnerships to bring a genuinely new kind of living room device to market. Product Experience The core challenge was making four entertainment devices, a sound bar, voice assistants, and streaming services feel like one seamless system. I designed the interaction model end-to-end — input switching, universal search, playback control, and voice — all unified under a single remote with no mode switching. Onboarding Setup was one of the hardest problems. I led the design of the onboarding flow — guiding users through connecting devices, mapping inputs, and pairing the remote in a way that felt approachable without requiring a manual. Getting first-run right was critical to retention and reducing support load. Watch Experience I designed the Watch experience — the core TV UI users see when browsing and selecting content. The goal was a unified guide that understood what services and devices you had, surfaced the right content at the right time, and made it feel like everything just worked together. Store I also led the in-product Store experience, giving users a way to discover and add new channels and apps directly from the TV — extending the platform without requiring a phone or laptop. Demo
