TuneIn In-Dash Carmode

ui design · visual design · ux design

In order to many TuneIn as ubiquitous as the FM receiver we needed to have a turnkey auto solution. The following screenshots are just part of a comprehensive package that I designed to deliver to any auto manufacturer that we did business with.

Auto implementations are notoriously difficult to work through. The safety demands are high, and the integrations painstaking. Furthermore the auto industry space is currently in a state of flux; there is no "right" way to design something for the in dash unit.

Therefore I often found that these designs became the cornerstone of many design and safety conversations. Our goal was to create a completely easy to use app that would not distract a driver yet give them as much of what they wanted as we could provide. We defended out position against many oems that asked for more features, and more distraction and became industry leaders in the digital audio in-dash space.

To date I have worked with GM, Volvo, Nissan, Chrysler, Mercedes Benz, Chevy and more.
