Boone Spooner

reel | product | spotify | about

Boone Spooner is a product manager and composer. He led Android Safety products at Google, including Android Earthquake Alerts and Emergency Location Service. He was Principal Product Manager at Caavo, where he launched the Caavo Classic and Caavo Control Center. He served as Senior Director of Product at TuneIn, where he launched TuneIn Premium, the NFL audio partnership, and audiobook integrations. He is also a composer and music producer with music available on Spotify.
