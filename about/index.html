I'm a product manager and composer based in the US. My work sits at the intersection of technology, design, and audio — building products that reach people at critical moments, and making music that does the same.

On the product side, I've led safety infrastructure at Google, including Android Earthquake Alerts — the world's largest earthquake detection network, using 2+ billion Android phones as seismometers — and Emergency Location Service, which delivers precise location to emergency services automatically when 911 is dialed. I co-authored a peer-reviewed paper on the earthquake system published in Science magazine.

Before Google, I was Principal Product Manager at Caavo, where I led the launch of both Caavo Classic and the Caavo Control Center — a new kind of universal remote that unified every device and streaming service in your living room. Prior to that, I was Senior Director of Product at TuneIn, where I launched TuneIn Premium, the NFL audio partnership, and the company's audiobook integration, among other things.

Earlier in my career I led design and product across Android, iOS, Google Home, Chromecast, Android Auto, Android TV, and Apple Watch at TuneIn — a period of intense platform expansion and one of the most prolific design stretches of my life.

Alongside product, I've worked as a composer and music producer throughout my career. My music is on Spotify.
